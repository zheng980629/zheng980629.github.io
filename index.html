Biography

I am a 3rd-year PhD candidate at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Feng Zhao. Currently, I am a research associate in MMLab@NTU at Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. Before that, I got a B.E. degree at Nanjing Agricultural University in 2020.

My research interests focus on computer vision, especially low-level vision tasks and multi-modal image fusion.
